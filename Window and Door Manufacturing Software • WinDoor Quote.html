Features

Review some of the most important WinDoors Quote featues below!

CLOUD BASED QUOTING

Fast and accurate web based ERP and Quoting software for window, door, screen and partition manufacturers. Mobile responsive design with DocuSign integration allows sales reps to quote and sell projects at the customer’s home and present a professional proposal all in one visit.

CNC INTEGRATION

Automatically generate and communicate order data directly to the CNC machining centers and saws via our API, eliminating the need to manually create and transfer production files via USB. WDQ also generates linear cut and binning lists that are optimized for maximum material and production efficiency.

PRODUCTION SCHEDULING

Schedule manufacturing tasks using a graphical user interface that shows employee and machinery utilization based upon time data for specific tasks for each product manufactured.  The production time for every task for each unique order is automatically and individually calculated based upon detailed configuration rules.

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

Accurately manage your company’s inventory through an automated bill of materials which generates precise job costing data and automatically reduces inventory based upon that specific job’s usage.

PAYROLL REPORTING

Easily manage your company’s payroll with our cloud based time clock system. Generate payroll reports that accurately calculate regular and overtime pay based upon your pay period all while eliminating false timecard punches.

LOGISTICS

Instantly quote and ship your LTL shipments using our integration with over 50 national and regional trucking companies.  Efficiently send your parcel shipments using our UPS and FedEx integration.

SALES iQ

Our CRM helps your sales staff manage your company’s relationship with prospective and active customers while providing timely insight for management.  Sales iQ includes a full email marketing campaign system which enables you to send automated, personalized email to any contact.

DAILY BACKUP

Never lose your company’s valuable data with daily backups to our cloud based hosting package on SSD SQL servers. At then end of the day, each customer’s data is backed up at two remote server locations, bringing peace of mind to your employees.

PRICING

Dial in precise pricing for each of your products and customers. From different glass configurations to two tone colors to mass price changes, manufacturers can quickly and easily update product pricing using our Window and Door Quoting Software
